9. Expenses and Travel (Part 1)

We use Ramp for expense management. Review the Ramp Policies and company expense guide.

Key Policies

  • Per Diem: Up to $60/day for meals/entertainment (itemized receipts required).
  • Gratuities: Reimbursed at 15–20% (meals), 10% (taxi/rideshare).
  • Mileage & Tolls: Business mileage reimbursed; commuting not reimbursed.
  • Phone Reimbursement: $50 monthly via Ramp.

9. Expenses and Travel (Part 2)

Travel Booking & Payment

  • Booking Tool: All travel must be booked through Concur (SAP).
  • Booking Fee: $11.00 per trip (allocate to facility, no receipt needed).
  • Payment: Charge all expenses to your Ramp Card.
  • Reconciliation: Via Ramp App (receipts required, except Concur fee).

Prohibited Expenses

  • Upgrades (airline/hotel/rental), personal items/snacks.
  • Health club memberships, spa services.
  • Traffic fines, parking violations.

11. Visiting a Facility

Professional Presence

  • Remember you are a guest; maintain professionalism and respect.
  • Check-In/Check-Out: Report to facility leadership on arrival/departure.
  • Flexibility: Adapt respectfully to each facility's processes.

12. Safety & Compliance

Ask local leadership about site-specific practices before walking the building.

Infection Control

  • Dress Code: Professional attire; avoid open-toed shoes in care areas.
  • Hygiene: Always wash/sanitize hands when entering/exiting/moving between areas.
  • PPE: Follow signage and ask leadership about current PPE expectations.

Reporting Concerns

  • Report compliance/safety concerns to facility leadership.
  • Escalate significant/unresolved concerns to your supervisor or HR.

13. HIPAA: Protecting Privacy

HIPAA applies to everyone—including visitors. Protect resident information!

Core Directives

  • Never share or discuss resident information in public areas.
  • Do not take photos or videos inside the facility unless explicitly authorized.
  • Access only the information necessary for your role.
